PathBridge

AI-Assisted Rural Career Guidance Platform

Aligned with SDG 4 – Quality Education


Problem Overview

Many rural and government school students lack structured career awareness, scholarship knowledge, and mentorship access. Career decisions are often based on limited information.

Initial Survey Insights

  • 70% unaware of diploma pathways
  • 60% unaware of scholarship portals
  • 75% rely only on family advice

Future Scope

  • Regional language support (Tamil)
  • District-level institution mapping
  • Backend database integration
  • AI-based aptitude testing
